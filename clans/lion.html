Year 800

Foundation & Kingdom Building

Goal: Establish your kingdom economy, build your core production buildings, and start generating Livability bonuses. Lion's unique mechanic revolves around Livability — the more you upgrade zones, the more specialist workers you can assign.

1
Build Scout Camp
Scout all adjacent tiles. Lion's kingdom mechanics reward dense, upgraded zones — find good tiles for your core production buildings.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Prioritize food tiles and lore stones. Lion's Livability system means you want to upgrade zones as you colonize them — plan your expansion for dense, upgradeable tiles.
20 Food
3
Build core production buildings
Build food production, Woodcutter's Lodge, and a Trading Post. Lion's economy scales with the number of specialist workers you can assign — upgrade zones to increase Livability and assign more workers.
150 Wood
🦁

Livability: Lion's unique mechanic. Livability establishes the upper limit for specialist workers in a zone. Upgrading zones increases Livability, allowing you to assign more workers to production buildings. Focus on upgrading your core zones early.

4
Upgrade your starting zone
Upgrading zones increases Livability. Start with your Town Hall zone — more Livability means more workers on food and wood production, accelerating your early economy.
Stone + Wood
5
Build Docks or Trading Post
Steady Krown income funds your warchief and building upgrades. Lion's economy is flexible — adapt to your map.
80 Wood
6
Summon Lothaire
Lothaire is your warchief — a powerful frontline fighter. Get him out as soon as you have the resources. His presence boosts your military significantly.
Krowns + Iron
🦁

Zone upgrades are your priority: Lion's entire economy revolves around Livability. Upgrade zones as fast as possible to increase your specialist worker cap. A fully upgraded Lion economy is one of the strongest in the game.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame, keep upgrading zones, and build out your military presence. Lion's economy compounds with each zone upgrade — the more you upgrade, the stronger you get.

1
Upgrade more zones
Keep upgrading zones to increase Livability. Each upgrade allows you to assign more specialist workers, compounding your production output.
Stone + Wood
2
Build Forge & upgrade tools
Upgrade food production and Sailors. Lion's economy scales well with tool upgrades and Livability bonuses.
60 Wood Iron + Krowns
3
Colonize aggressively
Expand toward valuable tiles. Upgrade each zone as you colonize it to maximize Livability and production output.
Food per tile
4
Build Training Camp & recruit military
Build a Training Camp and start converting villagers. Lion can field any standard unit — choose based on your team composition.
50 Wood
5
Feast in November
Accelerate your Fame gain. Lion's zone upgrades generate Fame — the more you upgrade, the faster you hit 200 Fame.
Food varies
🦁

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Feeling Safe — +3 Happiness with warchief, +1 per upgraded military camp
3rd Carpentry Mastery — –20% building upgrade costs and upkeep
4th Recruitment — +25% permanent population growth speed
Year 802

Military Build-Up & Full Economy

Goal: Build out your military force, maximize zone upgrades, and push toward 500 Fame. Lion's fully upgraded economy is one of the strongest in the game.

1
Convert 7+ military units
Lion can field any standard unit. Choose based on your team composition. Warriors for frontline, Axe Throwers for ranged, Shield Bearers for defense.
Krowns + Food
2
Upgrade all zones to maximum Livability
A fully upgraded Lion economy with maximum Livability in all zones is one of the strongest economic positions in the game. Prioritize this above all else.
Stone + Wood
3
Build Altar of Kings
Unlocks additional lore paths. Lion's lore tree has powerful economic and military bonuses — prioritize the paths that complement your team composition.
Wood + Stone
4
Push the enemy
With a fully upgraded economy and strong military, Lion is ready to push. Use Lothaire to lead your army and target enemy production zones.
Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ military units, keep your zone upgrades maxed, and use Lion's economic advantage to outlast the enemy.

1
Sustain 10+ military units in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle Krown income into maintaining your warband. Lion's economy should be generating significantly more resources than the enemy by this point.
2
Coordinate with team to close out
Lion is a strong late-game carry. If your team has early-game clans, they should have created space for you to scale. Now it's time to deliver.
Year 804

Endgame Domination

Goal: Close out with Domination, Fame, or Trade Victory. Lion's versatile economy makes all victory conditions achievable.

1
Military surge with fully upgraded units
Lion's economy should be generating significantly more resources than the enemy. Use this advantage to field a larger, more upgraded army.
2
Push for your chosen victory condition
Lion can win via Domination, Fame, or Trade. Choose based on your map position and team composition. Your economic advantage makes all three achievable.
