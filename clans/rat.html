Year 800

Foundation & Eir

Goal: Get Eir on the map immediately, start clearing neutral tiles to expand and get stone, and establish food + wood income before winter hits.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout out all tiles connected to your Town Hall. Assign Scouts to Town Hall to gather food.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
You can expand while scouting if you see a Lore Stone or any Ruins(Tombs or Shipwrecks). Otherwise wait until you see Lore or Food.
20 Food
3
Build Food Buildings and Woodcutter's Lodges.
Your population grows with how many buildings have been built. You NEED to constantly be building.
80-130 Wood
🐾

Recruiting your Chief:150 Krowns is what you need to recruit Eir. Additionally you need 10 wounded villagers.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Build a second Woodcutter's Lodge for more population. Focus on food production and building out.
50 Wood
5
First Lore and Shaman Camp
Build a Shaman Camp and take Sharp Axes. You'll want Healing Fire so you can Overwork as much as possible.
60 Wood
🦌

Lore priority summary for 801:

1st Sharp Axes +20% Wood production
2nd Healing Fire When Purification Pyre are burning, they heal nearby units outside combat. This effect is 50% less effective during Overwork.
3rd Medicine Increases your healing speed by 50%.
4th Overcrowding You can place up to two buildings of the same type in a zone, except Defense Tower.
6
Build Docks/Trading Post
You need 150 Krowns to summon Eir. If you don't have a Ruins or Shipwreck you'll need to start your economy early.
80 Wood
7
Build a Stone Mine
Shaman Camp Upgrades are ESSENTIAL. Mine in winter of 800 and don't stop.
60 Wood
🐾

Clearing priority: Killing Neutrals give you Pyre to heal your units.

⚠

Upgrade Town Hall your population grows rapidly and you want to build plenty of Shaman Camps and other Buildings.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame as fast as possible to unlock the Shamanic Care. Establish your krown economy and build out a sustainable mid-game.

1
Build Longship Dock(s)/ Trading Post
Longships can be good for Lore but you need lots of Krowns so you can go Marketplace. With Overcrowding build at least 2 Trading posts on the same tile.
80 Wood
2
Scouting and Mining
Start mining Iron. You'll want to upgrade your Merchants, Shaman and food producers. If you have enough Iron build Rat's relic.
60 Wood
3
Colonize aggressively & Clear
Expand toward wolf dens and deer tiles. If your team needs it clear them, the more you expand the harder your Shaman will scale when you're ready to convert.
Food per tile
5
Overwork Constantly
As often as you can overwork. Convert 2-4 low villagers to Shaman once you've gotten Enrollment.
7
More Stone
If you have access to more stone nodes build more Mines. Even if you don't use it all you can trade it for good economy.
60 Wood
Year 802

Full Shaman Convert & Military Build-Up

Goal: Mass convert Shamans, upgrade Shaman Camps and Food Buildings, and prepare to contest or rush enemy positions. This is your power window.

1
Build Forge & upgrade tools
Upgrade Woodcutters, Hunters, Sailors, and Trackers. Tool upgrades compound your existing production bonuses. With Spoils of Plenty active, Hunter tool upgrades cost less.
80 Wood Iron + Krowns
2
Upgrade your Shaman Camps
You should have plenty of Gold and Wood for upgrading. Get ready to attack the enemy. Your upkeep per year just became much more expensive so you want to finish out the game.
Krowns + Stone Wood
3
Upgrade Food buildings and Woodcutters Lodge
Upgrade 1 lodge and 1-2 Food buildings.
Wood Stone + Krowns
⚔

Rush vs. Scale decision point: 28 Population should be an easy reach by this point.11-14+Shaman Warband is what you're aiming for. You should be able to grind down any other military by this point.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 14+ Shaman, keep Overworking, and use Eir's Blood Ties to decisively win battles.

1
Sustain 14+ Shaman in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle gold income directly into maintaining this number. Trackers with full trophy upgrades are one of the strongest sustained ranged forces in the game at this point.
2
Continue Overworking
Be careful to only Overwork if there are 1-2 workers who will die during the overwork. Convert them for cheaper to fuel your army.
3
Forge your Relic
At this point forge your relic. You get a great tank that deals a lot of damage and heals quickly.
100 Krowns + 10 Iron 100 Wood
⚠

Watch for axe throwers: By 803–804, enemy ax throwers become a serious threat — they out-damage your Shaman. Rush the backline with Blood Ties you out sustain in almost every battle.

Year 804

500 Fame & Endgame

Goal: Reach 500 Fame

1
Unlock Proliferation (500 Fame milestone)
Your 500 Fame milestone gives you more population storage and longer Purification Pyre.
500 Fame
2
Convert all you can
Your Shamans should have over 100 health each at this point. Look to have 20+ warband.
~800 Krowns
4
Push for victory condition
Rat wins most naturally via Dominion (player removal). Keep Overworking.
