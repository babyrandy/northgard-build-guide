Year 800

Foundation & First Brawlers

Goal: Get a Boarskin and Bearskin on the map immediately, start clearing neutral tiles for levels, and establish wood income before winter hits.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout out all tiles connected to your Town Hall.
50 Wood
3
Build House and Brawler Arena
Build a House and Brawler Arena. Immediately send 2 villagers into the Arena. Once they've finished fighting select Boar Soul and send Brawler
100 Wood
2
Expand Out and Brawler Arena
You want more Brawler Arenas. Expand and get another Arena down. Immediately send 2 more villagers into the arena. Choose Bear this time.
20 Food50 Wood
🐾

Microing and Lore:Your first lores will be Bloodthirst and Living Legends. Make sure your Bearskin is taking aggro. When Bearskin is low press "E" and select bearskin and move them back until your Boarskin is targetted.

4
Experience Gain
Select your Boarskin and make them absorb all carcasses until they reach Level 2. Afterwards have your Bearskin Absorb everything.
5
Expansion and Houses
Absorbing carcasses with Bear gives Wood. Be sure to Build houses with this to keep your population growing.
50 Wood
6
Arenas and Foxskin
Your next Brawler will fight a fox soul. This gives you Krowns when you absorb carcasses.
50 Wood
7
Clear the Map
You will be stronger than any other clan this early. Use this to keep pressure off your teammates. Clear neutrals and make sure to continue to build out and make more Brawler Arenas.
🐾

Clearing priority: Wolves and foxes will be the easiest. If there is a lone Bear or Boar that will be far easier than 6-9 foxes.

⚠

Money Making: you won't be making any supporting buildings. Be sure to give soe carcasses to your fox so you can recruit more Brawlers and keep up with upkeep.

Year 801

Clear & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame as fast as possible to get more Experience for kills and partial XP for dead Brawlers.

1
More Arenas
By this point you should be on the way to get a 4th or 5th Brawler arena. Get a second Bearskin and all other addtions should be Foxskins.
50 Wood
2
Take your 3rd Lore & more
Now go down the middle tree. The most important Lores are "Horde Instict" and "Military Strategy"
Lore
🦌

Lore priority summary for 801:

1st Horde Instict — When multiple berserkers of the same type fight alongside, their abilities are stronger, and they gain more Military Experience.
2nd Monster Slayer — Improves yourd military units' attack by 10% against Draconic units and by 30% against other mystical creatures. Also increases Military Experience gains by 50% for each mystical creature killed.
3rd Military Strategy Military units gain +5% attack for each type of friendly military unit in the zone (max +25%).
4th Fur Coats Your units no longer have reduced power during winter.

Note: If your team is ready to convert take Battle Scars over Monster Slayer.

3
Colonize aggressively & Clear
Expand toward wolf dens and neutrals. While you're expanding clear your teammates.
Food per tile

Putting Pressure If you have vision of 2-3 of the enemy's tiles put pressure on them. If they have to waste resources on fighting you the better your team will perform when they convert.

5
Build Econ Buildings
At this point start economy buildings if your team can't convert(farmland, wood cutters, trading post, & mines).
Wood varies
Year 802

Full Brawler Convert & Smithy Upgrades

Goal: Mass convert Brawlers into Skins, make upgrades in the Smithy, and prepare to contest or rush enemy positions. This is your power window.

1
Convert up to 7 Brawlers
If you have been building as many Arenas as you can begin converting more to back up your main force.
300 Gold
2
Build Forge & upgrade tools
Upgrade Food production. Begin working on your Artifact: Kveldulf's Coat.
80 Wood Iron + Gold
3
Begin your assault.
Pressure the enemy team to use more resources than they want. If they bring a large force your Skins are faster than most military units.

Learn to micro well. You do not want your small force to die.

5
Upgrade Buildings
At this point your clan will start to suffer moral debuffs. Upgrade your Town Hall (and houses if need be).
Wood + Stone
⚔

Rush vs. Scale decision point: If the map is an 802 game (enemy rushes expected), push towards them. Hound is very aggressive and good at making the enemy commit due to your small warband. Otherwise continue building up your economy.

Year 803

Game Close Out & Falling Off

Goal: Push your team to close out the game. You won't scale as hard as late game clans

1
Harass and Expand
Keep up with your Houses. Build a Brewery to increase your happiness to push out more Brawlers. Continue to assault the enemy. If you can get them to over extend your team will be in a good spot to clean up for you.
⚠

Enemy Scaling: If your team can't close out the game in 802 you'll have a hard time winning in 803. Good micro can turn the tide of any battlefield.

Year 804

Team Close Out

Goal: Rely on your Lategame faction teammates to close out. (Stag, Owl, Lion, or Dragon)

1
Supporting your team.
Keep an eye on what your team is building. If they build out an army of axe throwers build out an army of Bearskins.
