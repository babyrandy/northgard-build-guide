Year 800

Foundation & Loot Pouches

Goal: Establish food and wood income, start scouting aggressively, and begin generating Loot Pouches to support your teammates. Eagle's versatility means you can adapt to whatever your team needs.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout all adjacent tiles — Eagle gains Lore when scouting new zones and Fame when discovering Neutral Factions and enemy Town Halls.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Prioritize food tiles and lore stones. Eagle starts with +75 Food, Wood, and Krowns — use this head start to expand faster than other clans.
20 Food
3
Build House + Food Production
Build a House and a food building. Eagle's Hall of Skalds replaces the Brewery and produces additional Fame alongside Happiness — build it early for Fame acceleration.
100 Wood
🦅

Starting bonus: Eagle starts with +75 Food, +75 Wood, +75 Krowns, and +40 Lore. Use this head start to build faster and scout more aggressively than other clans in the first few months.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Assign two villagers. Wood is needed for more buildings throughout 801.
50 Wood
5
Build Hall of Skalds
Replaces the Brewery. Produces additional Fame alongside Happiness. Eagle gains 50% more Fame and Happiness from territory — the Hall of Skalds amplifies this further.
60 Wood
6
Build Docks or Trading Post
Steady Krown income funds Grif and building upgrades. Eagle's economy is flexible — adapt to your map.
80 Wood
7
Summon Grif
Grif is your warchief — she attacks similarly to Signy but starts with higher base stats. Get her out as soon as you have the resources.
Krowns + Iron
🦅

Fame acceleration: Eagle gains 50% more Fame and Happiness from territory. Colonize aggressively and keep your Hall of Skalds running to hit 200 Fame faster than almost any other clan.

⚠

Build houses: Eagle's population growth is your economic engine. Don't neglect housing — you want the population headroom to convert military units in 801–802.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame to unlock annual supply bonuses. Eagle's 200 Fame bonus gives you +50 Food, Wood, and Krowns, +25 Lore, and +5 Stone every time a boat arrives — a massive economic boost.

1
Build Longship Dock(s)
Eagle's 200 Fame bonus triggers when a boat arrives — more docks means more frequent supply deliveries. Build 2–3 docks if you have coastal tiles.
80 Wood
2
Continue Lore & Build Forge
Build a Forge and start upgrading your Sailors and food production. Eagle's economy scales well with tool upgrades.
60 Wood
3
Colonize aggressively & Scout
Eagle gains Lore when scouting new zones and Fame when discovering Neutral Factions. Keep your scouts active — every new zone discovered is free Lore and Fame.
Food per tile
4
Feast in November
Accelerate your Fame gain. Eagle's 50% Fame bonus from territory means feasts hit harder than for other clans.
Food varies
5
Build Training Camp & recruit military
Eagle can field any standard military unit. Build a Training Camp and start converting villagers based on what your team needs — Warriors for frontline, Axe Throwers for ranged.
50 Wood
🦅

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Feeling Safe — +3 Happiness with warchief, +1 per upgraded military camp
3rd Recruitment — +25% permanent population growth speed
4th Carpentry Mastery — –20% building upgrade costs and upkeep
Year 802

Military Build-Up & Support

Goal: Build out your military force, upgrade your economy, and support your team with Loot Pouches and supply deliveries. Eagle is a versatile support-carry hybrid.

1
Convert 7+ military units
Eagle can field any standard unit. Choose based on your team composition — Warriors for frontline, Axe Throwers for ranged, Shield Bearers for defense.
Krowns + Food
2
Upgrade Training Camp
More warband slots means more units in a single engagement. Carpentry Mastery lore reduces upgrade costs by 20%.
Stone + Wood
3
Upgrade food buildings and Woodcutters
Eagle's economy scales well with upgrades. Upgrade 1–2 food buildings and your Woodcutter's Lodge to sustain your growing warband and supply deliveries.
Stone + Wood
4
Build Altar of Kings
Unlocks additional lore paths and the Hlidskjalf relic — which lets you recruit Einherjar, powerful warriors whose strength scales with your Fame. Eagle's Fame generation makes this relic extremely powerful.
Wood + Stone
⚔

Hlidskjalf relic: Eagle's unique relic lets you recruit Einherjar — powerful warriors whose strength scales with your clan's Fame. With Eagle's 50% Fame bonus from territory, Einherjar become significantly stronger than for other clans. Prioritize this relic.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ military units, keep supply deliveries flowing, and use Einherjar to dominate late-game engagements.

1
Sustain 10+ military units in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle Krown income into maintaining your warband. Eagle's supply deliveries help offset upkeep costs.
2
Reach 500 Fame: Annexation
At 500 Fame, unit production increases by 3% for every 200 Fame you have (max +15%). This compounds with Eagle's Fame generation to create a significant military advantage.
3
Recruit Einherjar from Hlidskjalf
Einherjar consume more food but their strength scales with your Fame. At high Fame levels, they're among the strongest units in the game. Keep your Fame high to maximize their power.
⚠

Food management: Einherjar consume significantly more food than standard units. Make sure your food production can support them before recruiting too many. Upgrade food buildings before scaling your Einherjar count.

Year 804

1000 Fame & Endgame

Goal: Reach 1000 Fame to unlock Valorous Leader for Grif. Eagle is one of the strongest late-game clans when properly scaled through Fame.

1
Unlock Valorous Leader (1000 Fame)
Grif gains the Valorous Leader ability — allied military units in the same zone deal +10% damage, and Grif's defense increases by 50% and health by 40%. This is a massive combat buff.
1000 Fame
2
Military surge with Einherjar + standard units
A mixed army of Einherjar and standard units with Grif's Valorous Leader buff is extremely powerful. Push for Domination or Fame victory.
