Year 800

Foundation & Meals

Goal: Get Andhrimnir on the map immediately if you don't have a clear clan, begin Venison & Ratatouille and Fish Soup, and establish food + wood income before winter hits.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout out all tiles connected to your Town Hall. Assign Scouts to Town Hall to gather food.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
You can expand while scouting if you see a Lore Stone or any Ruins(Tombs or Shipwrecks). Otherwise wait until you see Lore or Food.
20 Food
3
Build House and Food production
If you started with a ship wreck build a Stove. Otherwise build a House and Food production(Farmer, Fisherman) so your population can continue to expand.
50-130 Wood
🐾

Stoves: Chefs on Stoves generate additional ingredient for your meals. Pump out as many meals as possible to reach your 200 fame bonus.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Delay this until you have around 80 Wood remaining so you can squeeze extra food-gathering time first. Assign two villagers. Wood is needed for buildings throughout 801.
50 Wood
5
First Lore and Stove
Take Kitchen Nightmare if you don't have a clear clan, otherwise take Sharp Axes and continue into the top path. Build a Stove. Continue to build food buildings, you need to stockpile resources for the winter.
80 Wood
6
Clear and Expand
If you can, colonize dens and graveyards. You want 200 Fame ASAP.
40-80 Food
7
Build Mine and Wood Cutter's Lodge
Build a second Wood Cutter's Lodge. As soon as you have enough Wood build a Mine and start mining stone. Make sure you don't get Housed.
100 Wood10 Krown
🦌

Lore priority summary for 801:

1st Sharp Axes +20% Woodcutter production
2nd Stockpiling You gain +1 Food/Wood for each 100 Food/Wood you have in stock. Food Silos don't require a building slot.
3rd Recruitment Permanently increases population growth speed by 25%. You gain an additional 5% during 4 months for each unit from your clan killed by an enemy clan or sacrificed.
4th Cooking Mastery Meal have improved effects. Meal Food cost is reduced by 20%.
🐾

Meal priority: Venison & Ratatouille is your first Meal. You need to stockpile for winter. Afterwards Fish Soup as much as possible. You want to reach the end of the top lore tree ASAP.

⚠

Upgrade Town Hall upgrading buildings give you additional Fame. You need 200 to cook meals for your allies.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame as fast as possible to unlock the feast discount. Establish your krown economy and build out a sustainable mid-game.

1
Build Longship Dock(s)
If you have 2+ coastal tiles, build a second docks for steady Krown income. Otherwise build a Trading Post. Krowns will allow you to convert when your team is ready.
80 Wood
3
Colonize aggressively & scout for your team.
Expand toward wolf and fox dens. As a support clan do your best to make space for your other clans to build a good econ.
Food per tile
5
Meal for your team.
The more you cook meals for your team the more you'll be ahead of the enemy.
Food varies
6
Build Healer's Hut
If you needed to bring out your Chief build a Healer's Hut. Otherwise build another Stove.
60 Wood
7
Build Iron Mine
Look to start upgrading your production workers.
50 Wood 10 Krowns
Year 802

Support & Scout

Goal: Push more meals, scout the enemies for your team, and begin upgrading your military units.

1
Build a Forge
This will allow you to improve your units. Upgrade your Sailors first, this will allow you to produce more Krowns.
60 Wood 15 Krowns
2
Upgrade Scout Camp & Build Food Silos
If you haven't already build as many Food Silos as possible. It will increase your food storage but also your production.
80 Wood
3
Upgrade Houses
By this time your happiness will begin to be affected by your houses. Upgrade them to allow you to continue expanding.
Stone + Wood
4
Build second Stone Mine
Only if you're not under rush pressure. Stone fuels your building upgrades. If an enemy is pushing early, hold off and keep villagers on food/military rather than stretching economy.
60 Wood
5
Build Military Buildings
Your army will never be as strong as other armies due to Weaponsmith being replaced. Build Shieldbearer camps and upgrade them first. As support you'll want to be the frontline.
50 Wood
6
Get Economics
By this point you should already have Economics. If not get it and go down the bottom tree for Lore
Lore
⚔

Rush vs. Scale decision point: If the map is an 802 game (enemy rushes expected), prioritize Shieldbearer upgrade and build military buildings. If you have space to scale, hold back and keep upgrading your units.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ Shieldbearers, keep cooking meals to support allies, and think about closing out with a military meal.

1
Sustain 10+ Shieldbearers in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle gold income directly into maintaining this number. You're role in conflicts is to be annoying to the enemy, target their backline with your Shieldbearers.
2
Continue meal cycle with teammates
Your team should be able to snowball from this point. Keep supporting them and communicate so you know when they want to go in.
⚠

Have an Army: Even if it isn't large it still helps your team. Meals are good but extra bodies can change the balance of a fight.

Year 804

500 Fame — Plot for the Throne & Endgame

Goal: Reach 500 Fame this will allow you to push out more military units.

1
Unlock Plot for the Throne (500 Fame milestone)
This allows your population to grow even quicker than other clans. You can surprise the enemy with a 16-18+ army for a final confrontation.
500 Fame
2
Push for victory condition
You should be pushing for a Domination Victory. In AI games you can try for the special meal victory but that should be started late 802 and your team should be looking to defend you.
🌿

Military Meals: Use the defensive meal to make the enemy Axe Throwers less deadly.
