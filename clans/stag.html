Year 800

Foundation & Fame Rush

Goal: Establish food and wood income, build the Hall of Skalds, and start your Fame engine. Stag gains 50% more Fame and Happiness from territory — colonize aggressively.

1
Build Scout Camp
Scout all adjacent tiles. Stag starts with +75 Food, Wood, and Krowns, and +40 Lore — use this head start to expand faster than other clans.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Prioritize food tiles and lore stones. Every territory you control gives +50% Fame and Happiness — more territory means faster Fame gain.
20 Food
3
Build Hall of Skalds
Replaces the Brewery. Produces additional Fame alongside Happiness. This is your core Fame engine — build it as early as possible and assign a villager.
60 Wood
🦌

Starting bonus: Stag starts with +75 Food, +75 Wood, +75 Krowns, and +40 Lore. Use this head start to build the Hall of Skalds and expand faster than other clans in the first few months.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Assign two villagers. Wood is needed for more buildings throughout 801.
50 Wood
5
Build Longship Docks
Assign Raiders to your Docks to farm Fame instead of Lore. Stag's Raiders generate Fame from raiding — this is one of your fastest Fame acceleration tools.
80 Wood
6
Summon Warchief
Get your warchief out as soon as you have the resources. At 1000 Fame, your warchief gains Valorous Leader — allied units in the same zone deal +10% damage.
Krowns + Iron
🦌

Fame is everything: Stag's entire kit revolves around Fame. Every territory gives +50% Fame and Happiness. Colonize aggressively, keep your Hall of Skalds running, and assign Raiders to farm Fame from your Docks.

⚠

Don't neglect military: Stag is a Fame-focused clan but you still need a military presence. Build a Training Camp in 801 and keep at least 5–7 units in your warband to deter early rushes.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame to unlock annual supply bonuses. At 200 Fame, each boat brings +50 Food, Wood, and Krowns, +25 Lore, and +5 Stone — a massive economic boost.

1
Build more Longship Docks
More Docks means more frequent supply deliveries at 200 Fame. Build 2–3 Docks if you have coastal tiles. Assign Raiders to farm Fame.
80 Wood
2
Colonize aggressively
Every territory gives +50% Fame and Happiness. Expand toward wolf dens and neutral factions — destroying and colonizing enemy dens gives significant Fame.
Food per tile
3
Feast in November
Feasts give Fame. With Stag's 50% Fame bonus from territory, feasts hit harder than for other clans. Coordinate with your team — if someone else feasts, use the food for expansion instead.
Food varies
4
Build Training Camp & recruit military
Build a Training Camp and start converting villagers. Stag can field any standard unit — choose based on your team composition.
50 Wood
5
Build Forge & upgrade tools
Upgrade Sailors and food production. Stag's economy scales well with tool upgrades and the supply deliveries from 200 Fame.
60 Wood Iron + Krowns
🦌

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Feeling Safe — +3 Happiness with warchief, +1 per upgraded military camp
3rd Recruitment — +25% permanent population growth speed
4th Colonization — –30% food cost to colonize new territories
Year 802

Military Build-Up & Reach 500 Fame

Goal: Build out your military force, forge the Hlidskjalf relic, and push toward 500 Fame to unlock Inspirational Tales — +3% unit production per 200 Fame.

1
Convert 7+ military units
Stag can field any standard unit. Choose based on your team composition. Warriors for frontline, Axe Throwers for ranged, Shield Bearers for defense.
Krowns + Food
2
Forge Hlidskjalf relic
Lets you recruit Einherjar — powerful warriors whose strength scales with your clan's Fame. With Stag's Fame generation, Einherjar become significantly stronger than for other clans.
Iron + Krowns
3
Upgrade Training Camp
More warband slots means more units in a single engagement.
Stone + Wood
4
Build Altar of Kings
Unlocks additional lore paths. At 500 Fame, Stag unlocks Inspirational Tales — unit production increases by 3% for every 200 Fame (max +15%). This compounds with your Fame generation.
Wood + Stone
⚔

Einherjar scaling: Einherjar's strength scales with your Fame. At 500+ Fame, they're significantly stronger than standard Warriors. Prioritize the Hlidskjalf relic and keep your Fame high to maximize their power.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ military units, keep supply deliveries flowing, and use Einherjar to dominate late-game engagements.

1
Sustain 10+ military units in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle Krown income into maintaining your warband. Supply deliveries from 200 Fame help offset upkeep costs.
2
Recruit Einherjar from Hlidskjalf
Keep your Fame high to maximize Einherjar strength. At 800+ Fame, they're among the strongest units in the game.
3
Coordinate with team to close out
Stag is a strong late-game carry. If your team has early-game clans (Wolf, Hound, Lynx), they should have created space for you to scale. Now it's time to deliver.
Year 804

1000 Fame & Endgame

Goal: Reach 1000 Fame to unlock Valorous Leader for your warchief. Stag is one of the strongest late-game clans when properly scaled through Fame.

1
Unlock Valorous Leader (1000 Fame)
Your warchief gains Valorous Leader — allied military units in the same zone deal +10% damage, and your warchief's defense increases by 50% and health by 40%.
1000 Fame
2
Military surge with Einherjar + standard units
A mixed army of Einherjar and standard units with Valorous Leader is extremely powerful. Push for Domination or Fame victory.
