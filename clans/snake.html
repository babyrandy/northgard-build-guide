Year 800

Foundation & Young Signy

Goal: Establish food and wood income, build Skirmisher Camps, and start clearing with Signy. She starts as a child and grows stronger each year — protect her early.

1
Build Scout Camp
Scout all adjacent tiles. Signy starts on the map with you — she can use Scorched Earth to burn neutral zones and steal resources immediately.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Prioritize food tiles. Snake doesn't need to be picky — you want territory to burn and resources to steal.
20 Food
3
Build Skirmisher Camp
Skirmishers are your primary military unit — fast, stealthy, and cheap. They can traverse neutral zones without engaging combat and attack enemy production buildings to steal resources.
50 Wood
🐍

Signy grows up: Signy starts as a child and gains new abilities each year. At age 16 (Year 801), she learns Cloaked — she can hide from enemies unless war is declared. At age 17, all military units learn Cloaked. At age 18, Scorched Earth can target enemy zones. Protect her early.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge + Food Production
Assign two villagers to wood and build a food source. Snake needs a stable economy to sustain Skirmisher upkeep.
100 Wood
5
Use Scorched Earth on neutral zones
Signy can burn neutral zones to gain military advantage and steal resources. Use this to deny resources to enemies and generate income for your clan.
6
Steal Lore from other clans
Snake can copy Knowledge from other clans they encounter instead of earning Fame. Use this to accelerate your lore progression without needing to generate Fame.
Stolen Lore
🐍

No Fame Victory: Snake cannot win via Fame. Instead, they copy Knowledge from other clans. Focus on Domination Victory — use Signy's stealth and Skirmishers to overwhelm enemies through sabotage and surprise attacks.

⚠

Protect Signy: Signy is your most valuable unit. She grows stronger each year — losing her early is devastating. Keep her away from large enemy forces until she's at least age 17.

Year 801

Signy Age 16 & Cloaked

Goal: Reach Year 801 to unlock Signy's Cloaked ability. She can now hide from enemies and move through zones undetected. This opens up aggressive harassment strategies.

1
Build more Skirmisher Camps
More Skirmisher Camps means more Skirmishers in your warband. Skirmishers are cheap and fast — you want 7–10 of them by mid-801.
50 Wood
2
Use Signy's Cloaked ability
At age 16, Signy can hide from enemies unless war is declared in that zone. Use this to scout enemy positions, steal resources, and set up ambushes without being detected.
3
Steal Lore from encountered clans
Keep stealing Lore from every clan you encounter. Snake's lore progression is entirely dependent on stealing — the more clans you encounter, the faster you advance.
Stolen Lore
4
Use Under Pressure lore
Skirmishers can attack enemy production buildings and houses to steal the resources they contain. This is your primary economic harassment tool — target enemy Forges and food buildings.
Lore
5
Build Docks or Trading Post
Steady Krown income funds Skirmisher upkeep and building upgrades.
80 Wood
🐍

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Under Pressure — Skirmishers can attack enemy production buildings to steal resources
3rd Fur Coats — Your units no longer have reduced power during winter
4th Shiny Happy People — –20% required Happiness based on population
Year 802

Signy Age 17 & Army-Wide Cloaked

Goal: Reach Year 802 for Signy's age 17 ability — all military units learn Cloaked. Your entire army can now move undetected. This is Snake's peak power window.

1
Deploy army-wide Cloaked
At age 17, Signy teaches Cloaked to all military units. Your entire warband can now move through zones undetected. Use this to set up devastating ambushes on enemy positions.
2
Use Scorched Earth on two zones simultaneously
At age 17, Signy can burn two zones at once. Use this to deny resources to multiple enemies simultaneously and create chaos in their economy.
3
Forge Gungnir relic
Grants all military units the Stealth ability and enhances their evasiveness. Combined with army-wide Cloaked, your army becomes nearly impossible to intercept.
Iron + Krowns
4
Push the enemy
With army-wide Cloaked and Gungnir, your Skirmishers can infiltrate enemy territory undetected. Target their Town Hall and production buildings for maximum disruption.
⚔

Ambush timing: Move your cloaked army into position before declaring war. When you attack, the enemy has no time to react — your Skirmishers are already inside their territory. This is Snake's signature play.

Year 803

Signy Age 18 & Enemy Zone Scorched Earth

Goal: Reach Year 803 for Signy's age 18 ability — Scorched Earth can now target enemy zones, giving military units a 50% attack buff in those zones.

1
Use Scorched Earth on enemy zones
At age 18, Signy can burn enemy zones. Military units in burned zones get a 50% attack buff. Use this before major engagements to devastate enemy defenses.
2
Sustain 10+ Skirmishers in warband
Keep Skirmisher Camps upgraded and cycle Krown income into maintaining your warband. Skirmishers with Gungnir's Stealth and Cloaked are extremely difficult to counter.
3
Coordinate with team to close out
Snake is a strong mid-game clan. If your team has late-game carries, use Signy's Scorched Earth to weaken enemy positions before your team's final push.
Year 804

Endgame Domination

Goal: Close out with Domination. Snake's stealth mechanics and Signy's full power make them a formidable endgame threat.

1
Burn enemy zones before major battles
Use Scorched Earth on enemy zones to give your units a 50% attack buff. Combined with Gungnir's Stealth, your army is devastating in burned zones.
2
Push for Domination Victory
Snake wins via Domination. Use your cloaked army to infiltrate enemy territory and destroy their Town Hall. Signy's full power at age 18 makes this achievable.
