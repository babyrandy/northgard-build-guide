Year 800

Foundation & Sindri's Forge

Goal: Get Sindri working immediately — he combines the roles of Miner, Smith, and Builder. Start forging and building faster than any other clan. Hippogriff's Varangian Guard is your military backbone.

1
Build Scout Camp
Scout all adjacent tiles. Look for Iron and Stone deposits — Sindri needs ore to forge. Hippogriff's forging speed is significantly faster than other clans.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Prioritize tiles with Iron and Stone deposits. Sindri's faster mining speed means you'll have ore significantly sooner than other clans.
20 Food
3
Build Forge & summon Sindri
Sindri is your warchief and master craftsman. He mines, forges, and builds faster than standard workers. Get him working on ore immediately.
60 Wood Krowns + Iron
🦅

Sindri: Sindri is the eldest of Brok and Eitria from the Horse clan. He combines their forging mastery with the discipline of Southern Kingdoms. He mines, forges, and builds faster than standard workers — keep him busy at all times.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge + Food Production
Assign two villagers to wood and build a food source. Hippogriff needs a stable economy to sustain Sindri's forging operations and Varangian Guard upkeep.
100 Wood
5
Build Iron Mine
Sindri needs Iron to forge. Build an Iron Mine immediately and assign him to it. His faster mining speed means you'll have Iron significantly sooner than other clans.
60 Wood 10 Krowns
6
Build Docks or Trading Post
Steady Krown income funds Varangian Guard recruitment and building upgrades.
80 Wood
🦅

Varangian Guard: Hippogriff's unique military unit. They're elite warriors with high stats, forged by Sindri's craftsmanship. Build Varangian Guard camps and recruit them as your primary military force.

Year 801

Economy & Varangian Guard

Goal: Build out your Varangian Guard force, keep Sindri forging upgrades, and establish a strong mid-game economy.

1
Build Varangian Guard camps
Recruit Varangian Guard — your primary military force. They're elite warriors with high stats, significantly stronger than standard Warriors.
50 Wood
2
Keep Sindri forging upgrades
Sindri's faster forging means you can upgrade tools significantly faster than other clans. Prioritize Merchant/Sailor upgrades first, then food production, then military.
Iron + Krowns
3
Colonize aggressively
Expand toward more Iron and Stone deposits. Sindri's faster mining means you can sustain more ore production than other clans.
Food per tile
4
Feast in November
Accelerate your Fame gain. Hippogriff's forging bonuses and Varangian Guard make Fame generation faster than most clans.
Food varies
🦅

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Feeling Safe — +3 Happiness with warchief, +1 per upgraded military camp
3rd City Builder — improving a zone is 50% cheaper; specialized units get +10% production and –50% upkeep
4th Carpentry Mastery — –20% building upgrade costs and upkeep
Year 802

Full Varangian Guard & Military Push

Goal: Build out your full Varangian Guard force, upgrade your military buildings, and push the enemy. Hippogriff's elite units are at their peak in 802.

1
Convert 7+ Varangian Guard
Your primary military force. Varangian Guard are elite warriors with high stats — significantly stronger than standard Warriors. Combined with Sindri's forging buffs, they're formidable.
Krowns + Food
2
Upgrade military buildings
More warband slots means more Varangian Guard in a single engagement. Sindri's faster building speed means upgrades complete significantly faster than for other clans.
Stone + Wood
3
Push the enemy
Varangian Guard with Sindri leading are a powerful military composition. Push into enemy territory and use Sindri's combat abilities to support your army.
Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ Varangian Guard, keep Sindri forging, and use Hippogriff's economic advantage to outlast the enemy.

1
Sustain 10+ Varangian Guard in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle Krown income into maintaining your warband. Varangian Guard are expensive but significantly stronger than standard units.
2
Keep Sindri forging
Never let Sindri sit idle. He should always be mining, forging, or building. His faster output is your most valuable economic asset.
3
Coordinate with team to close out
Hippogriff is a strong mid-to-late game clan. If your team has early-game clans, they should have created space for you to scale. Now it's time to deliver.
Year 804

Endgame Domination

Goal: Close out with Domination or Fame Victory. Hippogriff's Varangian Guard and Sindri's forging make them a formidable endgame threat.

1
Military surge with 14+ Varangian Guard
A full warband of Varangian Guard with Sindri is one of the most powerful military compositions in the game. Push for Domination victory.
2
Use Sindri in combat
Sindri is a powerful combat unit in addition to his crafting role. Use him to lead your Varangian Guard into battle.
