Year 800

Foundation & First Berserker

Goal: Get Egil on the map, start clearing neutrals for Krowns and food, and establish wood income before winter. Your first military unit is free — use it immediately.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout out all tiles connected to your Town Hall.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Expand to a food tile or lore stone. Wolf doesn't need to be picky — you want territory to clear.
20 Food
3
Build House + Training Camp
Build both immediately. Convert a villager to a Berserker right away — your first military unit is free. Send them clearing immediately.
100 Wood
🐺

Free first unit: Wolf's starting bonus gives you the first military unit for free. Don't delay — convert immediately and start clearing. Every kill generates Krowns via Spoils of War once you take that lore.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Assign two villagers. Wood is needed for more Training Camps and Houses throughout 801.
50 Wood
5
First Lore: Fur Coats
Your units no longer suffer reduced power during winter. This is critical for Wolf — you want to be aggressive year-round, not just in summer.
Lore
6
Build Docks or Trading Post
You need 150 Krowns + 5 Iron to summon Egil. Start your economy early. Docks are preferred if you have coastal tiles.
80 Wood
7
Summon Egil
Egil is a powerful frontline fighter. Get him out as soon as you have 150 Krowns and 5 Iron. He can clear harder camps that your Berserker can't handle alone.
150 Krowns + 5 Iron
🐺

Clearing priority: Clear wolf dens and fox burrows first — they give Krowns and food. Your Berserkers consume less food than normal units, so keep them active. Every kill builds toward Spoils of War income.

⚠

Don't neglect food: Wolf's military units consume less food, but you still need a stable supply. Build a food source before converting too many villagers to Berserkers.

Year 801

Clear & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame to unlock Egil's free colonization. Keep clearing neutrals and building Berserkers. This is your strongest window.

1
Build second Training Camp
More Training Camps means more Berserkers in your warband. Wolf scales with warband size — the more units you have, the more Happiness they generate.
50 Wood
2
Take Spoils of War Lore
Gain Krowns for each enemy unit killed. Eradicating a neutral faction earns 50 Military Experience. This turns your clearing into a self-sustaining economy.
Lore
3
Colonize aggressively & Clear for teammates
Expand toward wolf dens and enemy-adjacent tiles. Clear neutrals for your teammates — Wolf is the best early-game clear clan in the game.
Food per tile
4
Feast in November
Accelerate your Fame gain. After 200 Fame, Egil can colonize cleared zones for free every 12 months — a massive expansion tool.
Food varies
5
Build Healer's Hut
Keep Egil and your Berserkers alive during aggressive clearing pushes. Egil is your most valuable unit — don't let him die.
60 Wood
🐺

Lore priority for 801:

1st Fur Coats — Your units no longer have reduced power during winter
2nd Spoils of War — Gain Krowns per enemy kill; eradicating a faction earns 50 Military Experience
3rd Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
4th Legendary Heroes — +25% warchief attack and defense
Year 802

Full Berserker Push & Horn of Managarm

Goal: Mass convert Berserkers, forge the Horn of Managarm, and push the enemy. This is Wolf's peak power window — use it.

1
Convert 5–7 Berserkers
Your primary military force. Berserkers are fast, hit hard, and generate Happiness. Combined with Egil, this is a formidable early army.
Krowns + Food
2
Forge Horn of Managarm
Summons up to 3 White Wolves (50 Food each). Build the relic on a Wolf Den tile to halve the cost. White Wolves are powerful frontline tanks that draw enemy fire away from Egil.
50 Food per Wolf
3
Build Forge & upgrade tools
Upgrade food production and military units. Berserker upgrades increase their combat effectiveness significantly.
80 Wood Iron + Krowns
4
Push the enemy
Use Egil's Dominion to colonize cleared zones for free. Push into enemy territory — your +15% attack bonus outside friendly territory (500 Fame) makes you stronger on offense.
5
Take Conqueror Lore
+75% attack power against Defense Towers and 30% faster decolonization. This makes breaking enemy fortified positions much easier.
Lore
⚔

Rush vs. Scale: Wolf is an early-game clan. If you haven't pushed by 802, you're falling behind. Commit to aggression — your Berserkers and Egil are at their relative peak right now. Don't wait for 803.

Year 803

Sustained Pressure & Closing Out

Goal: Keep pressure on the enemy. Wolf falls off in late game — close out before 804 if possible.

1
Sustain 10+ Berserkers in warband
Keep your warband active and clearing. Berserkers generate Happiness — a larger warband means a happier clan and faster population growth.
2
Use Egil's Dominion aggressively
After 200 Fame, Egil can colonize cleared zones for free every 12 months. After 500 Fame, the cooldown drops by 50%. Use this to rapidly expand your territory.
3
Coordinate with team to close out
Wolf's job is to create chaos and pressure. If your team has late-game carries (Stag, Owl, Lion, Dragon), keep the enemy occupied so they can't scale.
⚠

Falling off: By 803–804, enemy clans with late-game scaling will outpace your Berserkers. Use your early advantage to end the game before they scale. Good micro and Egil's Dominion can still turn the tide.

Year 804

Team Close Out

Goal: Rely on your team's late-game carries. Support with Egil's Dominion, White Wolves, and Berserker harassment.

1
Keep Egil alive and active
Egil is your most valuable unit in 804. His Dominion ability and combat strength are still relevant even when Berserkers start to fall behind enemy units.
2
Use White Wolves as frontline tanks
Keep summoning White Wolves from the Horn of Managarm. They draw enemy fire and give your team's damage dealers time to work.
3
Support your team's push
Keep an eye on what your team is building. If they have axe throwers, bring Berserkers as frontline. If they have Warriors, use Egil to draw aggro.
