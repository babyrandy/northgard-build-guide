Year 800

Foundation & Ore Mining

Goal: Get 15 iron, start building up your economy for late game, and establish food + wood income before winter hits.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout out all tiles connected to your Town Hall.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Only expand out to 1–2 tiles. You want Colonization before expanding far.
20 Food
3
Build House, Woodcutter's Lodge, and another Scout Camp
If you started with a Shipwreck you can hold off on a Woodcutter's and immediately build a Mine on an Iron tile. Build a House so your population can continue to expand.
100 Wood 10 Gold
4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Delay this until you have around 80 Wood remaining. You'll want 3 Woodcutter's Lodges for your expansion — Ox is wood hungry.
50 Wood
5
First Lore and Food Buildings
Build your food building and take Sharp Axes as your first lore.
80 Wood
6
Set 3 Scouts on the Dwarves
Start building relations with them immediately. You want their operatives and Ores.
7
Infrastructure
Build up your infrastructure. If you can, build 3 Longship Docks for the Lore. Otherwise build 2–3 Trading Posts to build up your economy.
160 Wood
🌿

Lore Choice: You want Colonization as soon as possible to expand early game. Rely on your clear clan to keep neutrals off your back.

⛏

Mining Ores: You want to find a Dwarven Camp ASAP. If you don't have one nearby you'll need to build and operate your own Mines.

🦌

Lore priority for 800:

1st Sharp Axes — +20% Wood production
2nd Colonization — Reduce food needed to colonize by 30%
3rd City Builder — Improving a zone is 50% cheaper; specialized units get +10% production and –50% upkeep
4th Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
Year 801

Economy & Friendship with the Dwarves

Goal: Get Dwarves so you can mine and forge quicker.

1
Build Krowns & Military Camps
If you have 3 shorelines build solely Longship Docks. Otherwise build at least 1 Trading Post and 1 of each of the Military Buildings.
80 Wood
2
Continue Lore & Build Forge
You will now go down the middle tree. Build a Forge.
60 Wood
3
Colonize Aggressively
Look to get your Chief out. If you have a clear clan, expand toward Wolf Dens.
Food per tile
4
Forging Upgrades and Begin Relic
First queue up Merchants/Sailors & food production upgrades. Leadership is the military path you want — it benefits off all the forging. Your relic will make the enemy target only Torfinn, which is a game changer.
Iron + Krowns
5
Build Healer's Hut
You want to keep your Warriors healthy throughout engagements.
50 Wood
6
Go to Ancient Battlegrounds
Torfinn gains bonuses based on how many Battlegrounds he digs up. Start clearing neutrals to increase your military experience.
Year 802

Full Warrior Convert & Military Build-Up

Goal: Mass convert Warriors, upgrade your Food Buildings, and prepare to contest or rush enemy positions.

1
Convert up to 7 Warriors
300 gold funds 7 Warriors. This is your primary military force.
300 Gold
2
Upgrade Food Buildings
Upgrading your food buildings allows you to assign 2 villagers per building, increasing output significantly.
Stone Wood
3
Mine Stone and Iron
Continue to mine as much as possible. Your Dwarves should always be at work.
Iron Stone
4
Altar of Kings or Military Upgrades
If you have access to 3 stone think about Altar of Kings. Otherwise upgrade Military buildings.
Wood + Stone
5
Fight & Find Battlegrounds
Look to press the attack — your Chief (even with 1 Equipment) is a monster in battle. When Ancient Battlegrounds appear go straight for them. Your warriors are still scary on their own with Ferocious Charge.
⚔

Rush vs. Scale decision point: If the map is an 802 game (enemy rushes expected), push early with Torfinn leading. If you have space to scale, keep building Battleground stacks and convert more Warriors before your first major engagement.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ Warriors and use Torfinn's taunt to win decisively.

1
Sustain 10+ Warriors in Warband
You'll usually have fewer villagers than other clans. Look to maintain a large, healthy warband of 10+ Warriors at all times.
2
Press the Attack and Reach 500 Fame
Upgrade buildings and colonize zones to hit 500 Fame. You'll be able to destroy buildings and press the attack hard by this point.
3
Continue Feast Cycle
Coordinate with your team on feasts. Happiness bonuses keep your population growing and support faster warrior conversion to replace losses.
⚠

Watch for axe throwers: By 803–804, enemy axe throwers out-range your Warriors and attack very quickly. Use Torfinn's Taunt — the enemy will be helpless for the first 6 seconds of an engagement. Save this for a decisive battle.

Year 804

500 Fame — The True Hero & Endgame

Goal: Reach 500 Fame to unlock Torfinn's Building Destruction and close out the game.

1
Unlock The True Hero (500 Fame milestone)
If you haven't already, this allows you to quickly reach the enemy backline when fighting in their territory. Combo this ability with the Taunt for a devastating opening.
500 Fame
2
Military Surge with 14–16 Warriors
You shouldn't be going past 804 so going into a deficit is completely fine. Spend everything on your final push.
3
Push for Victory Condition
Ox wins most naturally via Dominion. Keep pushing to cripple the enemy team's economy and hold their territory.
🐂

Aegir timing: Save the ability for a decisive moment — either to break an enemy chokepoint or to defend your base during a siege.
