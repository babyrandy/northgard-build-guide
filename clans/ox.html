Year 800

Foundation & Ore Mining

Goal: Get 15 iron, start building up your economy for late game, and establish food + wood income before winter hits.

1
Build Scout Camp
Your very first build. Scout out all tiles connected to your Town Hall.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Only expand out to 1-2 tile. You want Colonization before expanding far.
20 Food
🌿

Lore Choice: You want Colonization as soon as possible to expand early game. Rely on your clear clan to keep neutrals off your back.

3
Build House, Wood Cutters Lodge, and another Scout Camp
If you started with a ship wreck you can hold off on a Wood Cutters and immediately build a Mines on an Iron tile. Build a House so your population can continue to expand.
100 Wood 10 Gold
🐾

Mining Ores:You want to find a dwarven camp ASAP. If you don't have one you'll need to build and operate your own mines.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge
Delay this until you have around 80 Wood remaining so you can squeeze extra food-gathering time first. You'll want 3 Woodcutters Lodges for your expansion.
50 Wood
5
First Lore and Food buildings
Build your Food building. Get Sharp Axes.
80 Wood
6
Set 3 scouts on the Dwarves
Start building relations with them immediately. You want their operatives and Ores.
7
Infrastructure
Build up your Infrastructure. Ox is Wood hungry so have at least 3 Woodcutters Lodges. If you can, build 3 Longship docks for the Lore. Otherwise build 2-3 Trading Posts to build up your economy.
160 Wood
🦌

Lore priority summary for 801:

1st Sharp Axes — +20% Wood production.
2nd Colonization — Reduce the amount of food needed to colonize by 30%.
3rd City Builder — Impoving a zone is 50% cheaper. Specialized units get 10% production increase and -50% upkeep
4th Weaponsmith — +20% to all military units attack.
Year 801

Economy & Reach friendship with Dwarves

Goal: Get dwarves so you can mine and forge quicker.

1
Build Krowns/Build Military camps
If you have 3 shorelines build solely Longship Docks. Otherwise build at least 1 Trading Post and 1 of each of the Military Buildings.
80 Wood
2
Future Lore & Forge
You will now go down the middle tree. Build a Forge.
60 Wood
3
Colonize aggressively
Look to get your Chief out. If you have a clear clan expand towards Wolf Dens.
Food per tile
5
Forging Upgrades and Begin Relic
First queue up Merchants/Sailors & Food production. Leadership is the military path you want to take to benefit off of all the forging. Your relic will make it so the enemy will only target Torfinn, this is a game changer.
Iron + Krowns
6
Build Healer's Hut
You want to keep your Warrior healthy.
50 Wood
7
Go to Ancient Battlegrounds
Torfinn gains bonuses based on how many Battlegrounds he digs up. Start clearing neutrals to increase your military experience.
Year 802

Full Warrior Convert & Military Build-Up

Goal: Mass convert Warriors, upgrade your Food Buildings, and prepare to contest or rush enemy positions.

1
Convert up to 7 Warriors
300 gold funds 7 Warriors. This is your primary military force.
300 Gold
3
Upgrade Food Buildings
Upgrading your food will allow you to assign 2 Villagers. Take the Archery Mastery lore if available — it cuts the upgrade cost by 50%. More slots means more Trackers in a single engagement.
Stone + Wood
4
Mine Stone and Iron
Continue to mine as much as possible. Your Dwarves should always be at work.
5
Think about Alter/Military
If you have access to 3 stone think about Altar of Kings. Otherwise upgrade Military buildings.
Wood + Stone
6
Fight & Find Battlegrounds
Look to press the attack, your Chief (even with 1 Equipment) is a monster in battle. When Ancient Battlegrounds appear go straight for them. Your warriors are still scary on their own with Ferocious Charge.
Lore
⚔

Rush vs. Scale decision point: If the map is an 802 game (enemy rushes expected), prioritize Fire Arrows and push early. If you have space to scale, hold back and keep converting Trackers until you have 14+ in your warband before engaging.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Map Control

Goal: Sustain 10+ Warriors and use Torfinn's taunt to win decisively.

1
Sustain 10+ Warriors in warband
You'll usually have less villagers than other clans. Look to have a large healthy warband of 10+ Warriors.
2
Press the Attack and 500 Fame
Upgrade buildings and colonize zones to get 500 fame. You'll be able to destroy buildings and press the attack hard by this point.
5
Use Kaelinn to clear Valkyries & Draugr Tombs
Kaelinn's mobility makes her excellent at taking down high-value neutral camps. If a camp is too strong for her alone, bring one Tracker for support. These camps drop significant resources and lore.
⚠

Watch for axe throwers: By 803–804, enemy ax throwers become a serious threat — they out range your Warriors and attack very quickly. Use Torfinn's Taunt: The enemy will be helpless for the first 6 seconds of an engagement. Make sure you use this ability in a decisive battle.

Year 804

500 Fame — The True Hero & Endgame

Goal: Reach 500 Fame to unlock Torfinn's Building Destruction.

1
Unlock The True Hero. (500 Fame milestone)
If you haven't already this will allow you to quickly reach the enemy backline if you're fighting in their territory. Combo this ability with the Taunt.
500 Fame
2
Military surge with 14–16 Warriors
You shouldn't be going past 804 so going into a deficit is completely fine.
3
Push for victory condition
Ox wins most naturally via Dominion. Keep pushing to cripple the other team's economy.
🌿

Aegir timing: Save the ability for a decisive moment — either to break an enemy chokepoint or to defend your base during a siege.
