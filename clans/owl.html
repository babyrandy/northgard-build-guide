Year 800

Foundation & Branches of Yggdrasil

Goal: Establish food and wood income, place your first Branches of Yggdrasil, and start spreading knowledge across the map. Owl's Norns scout faster and generate Lore — keep them active.

1
Build Scout Camp
Scout all adjacent tiles. Owl's Norns scout 100% faster than standard scouts — assign them immediately and keep them scouting throughout the game.
50 Wood
2
Expand Out
Prioritize food tiles and lore stones. Owl's Realm of Myrkviðr relic causes upgraded zones to grow a Forest and attract Deer and Fish — plan your expansion to maximize this.
20 Food
3
Place Branches of Yggdrasil
Branches of Yggdrasil spread clan-unique knowledge to zones they're placed in. Place them strategically to maximize their effect — they can be placed in any zone you control.
Lore
🦉

Norns: Owl's Norns scout 100% faster than standard scouts. After upgrading your Town Hall, they can explore enemy territories. You can assign up to 6 Norns and they don't count toward your population — they're free scouts.

4
Build Woodcutter's Lodge + Food Production
Assign two villagers to wood and build a food source. Owl needs a stable economy to sustain its Norn network and Branch placements.
100 Wood
5
Build Docks or Trading Post
Steady Krown income funds warchiefs and building upgrades. Owl's economy is flexible — adapt to your map.
80 Wood
6
Summon Skuld, Verdandi, or Urdr
Owl has three warchiefs — Skuld, Verdandi, and Urdr. You can recruit each separately. Each has unique abilities. Get at least one out in Year 800 to start benefiting from their combat and knowledge bonuses.
Krowns + Iron
🦉

Branches of Yggdrasil: These are Owl's core mechanic. Place them in zones to spread clan-unique knowledge. At 200 Fame, you can place Branches in enemy territory. At 500 Fame, zones can contain an additional Branch and Owl knowledges can be placed twice.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame to unlock Norns' ability to explore enemy territories and place Branches in enemy zones. This opens up aggressive knowledge spreading and scouting.

1
Upgrade Town Hall
After upgrading your Town Hall, Norns can explore enemy territories. This is critical for Owl — you need to know where to place your Branches for maximum effect.
Stone + Wood
2
Place Branches in enemy territory (200 Fame)
At 200 Fame, you can place Branches in enemy territory. Use your Norns to scout enemy positions, then place Branches in their most valuable zones to disrupt their economy.
3
Colonize aggressively
Expand toward valuable tiles. Owl's Realm of Myrkviðr relic causes upgraded zones to grow a Forest and attract Deer and Fish — upgrade zones as you colonize them.
Food per tile
4
Feast in November
Accelerate your Fame gain. Owl's knowledge spreading and Norn network make Fame generation faster than most clans.
Food varies
5
Recruit all three warchiefs
Owl can recruit Skuld, Verdandi, and Urdr separately. Each has unique abilities — having all three gives you significant combat and knowledge advantages.
Krowns + Iron each
🦉

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Feeling Safe — +3 Happiness with warchief, +1 per upgraded military camp
3rd Recruitment — +25% permanent population growth speed
4th Carpentry Mastery — –20% building upgrade costs and upkeep
Year 802

Military Build-Up & Realm of Myrkviðr

Goal: Forge the Realm of Myrkviðr relic, build out your military force, and push toward 500 Fame to unlock double Branch placement and doubled Owl knowledges.

1
Forge Realm of Myrkviðr relic
Upgraded zones grow a Forest and attract Deer and Fish, providing more Happiness. Upgrading a zone now costs 50 Lore instead of resources — a significant cost reduction for Owl's lore-heavy playstyle.
Iron + Krowns
2
Convert 7+ military units
Owl can field any standard unit. Choose based on your team composition. Warriors for frontline, Axe Throwers for ranged, Shield Bearers for defense.
Krowns + Food
3
Upgrade zones with Realm of Myrkviðr
With the relic, upgrading zones costs 50 Lore instead of resources. Upgrade your most valuable production zones to grow Forests and attract Deer and Fish.
50 Lore per upgrade
4
Reach 500 Fame: Pillars of Wisdom
At 500 Fame, zones can contain an additional Branch of Yggdrasil and Owl knowledges can be placed twice. This doubles your knowledge spreading capacity — a massive power spike.
500 Fame
⚔

Nomad Branches: In Conquest mode, Branches of Yggdrasil with clan-unique knowledge become "Nomad Branches" that can move. This makes Owl's knowledge spreading even more flexible and powerful in extended campaigns.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Knowledge Dominance

Goal: Sustain 10+ military units, keep your Norn network active, and use double Branch placement to dominate the knowledge landscape.

1
Sustain 10+ military units in warband
Keep military camps upgraded and cycle Krown income into maintaining your warband. Owl's three warchiefs provide significant combat bonuses.
2
Maximize Branch placement
With 500 Fame's double Branch placement, you can spread knowledge to twice as many zones. Place Branches in enemy territory to disrupt their economy and in your own zones to maximize production.
3
Coordinate with team to close out
Owl is a strong late-game carry. If your team has early-game clans, they should have created space for you to scale. Now it's time to deliver.
Year 804

Endgame Domination

Goal: Close out with Lore, Domination, or Fame Victory. Owl's knowledge spreading and three warchiefs make all victory conditions achievable.

1
Military surge with all three warchiefs
Skuld, Verdandi, and Urdr together provide massive combat bonuses. A full army with all three warchiefs is extremely powerful.
2
Push for Lore or Domination Victory
Owl wins most naturally via Lore Victory (unlock Ancestral Knowledge) or Domination. Keep your Norn network active and your Branches spreading knowledge.
