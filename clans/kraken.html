Year 800

Foundation & Coastal Economy

Goal: Establish your coastal economy, build Fishing Cabins, and start generating Wyrd from your Hörgr. Kraken is a coastal clan — prioritize coastal tiles above all else.

1
Build Scout Camp
Scout all adjacent tiles. Prioritize coastal tiles — non-coastal zones cost 50% more to colonize and reduce your attack power by 30%. Your entire strategy depends on coastal access.
50 Wood
2
Expand to Coastal Tiles
Only expand to coastal tiles in Year 800. Non-coastal colonization is 50% more expensive and weakens your units. Map your coastal path before expanding.
20 Food
3
Build Fishing Cabins
Replaces Longship Docks. 20% more effective than Fishing Huts and can fish in both lakes and the ocean. This is your primary food and Krown source — build as many as your coastal tiles allow.
80 Wood
🦑

Coastal dependency: Kraken cannot build Longship Docks and cannot pursue a Trade Victory. Your Fishing Cabins are your economy. Prioritize coastal tiles above all else — every non-coastal zone you colonize weakens your army by 30%.

4
Build Hörgr
Replaces the Brewery. Produces Wyrd to grant various bonuses and powers. You start with one Spectral Warrior — build more Hörgrs to recruit additional Spectral Warriors by maintaining a negative Wyrd balance.
60 Wood
5
Build Woodcutter's Lodge + Food Production
Assign two villagers to wood and build a food source. Kraken needs a stable economy to sustain Spectral Warriors and Kàra.
100 Wood
6
Summon Kàra
Kàra is your warchief — a Valkyrie with powerful combat abilities. She can regenerate health slowly when standing next to the Hörgr or in a high tide zone (after Valhalla Reinforcement lore). Get her out as soon as you have the resources.
Krowns + Iron
🦑

Spectral Warriors: You start with one Spectral Warrior. Build more Hörgrs and maintain a negative Wyrd balance to recruit additional ones. At 200 Fame, Spectral Warriors increase resource production by 15% in zones they inhabit — use them as economic units when not fighting.

Year 801

Economy & Reach 200 Fame

Goal: Hit 200 Fame to unlock Spectral Warriors' 15% production bonus and early event reveals. Build out your Spectral Warrior force and start pressuring the map.

1
Build more Fishing Cabins
More Fishing Cabins means more food and Krown income. Build as many as your coastal tiles allow — this is your primary economic engine.
80 Wood
2
Build more Hörgrs
More Hörgrs means more Spectral Warriors. Keep your Wyrd balance negative to maintain your Spectral Warrior count. Assign Spectral Warriors to production zones for the 15% bonus after 200 Fame.
60 Wood
3
Promote Seeresses to Valkyries
Seeresses can be promoted to Valkyries — powerful military units with special abilities. Valkyries are your primary combat force alongside Kàra and Spectral Warriors.
4
Feast in November
Accelerate your Fame gain. At 200 Fame, you feast whenever the Kraken emerges — a free feast that doesn't cost food.
Food varies
5
Build Forge & upgrade tools
Upgrade Fishermen and food production. Kraken's economy scales well with tool upgrades and Spectral Warrior production bonuses.
60 Wood Iron + Krowns
🦑

Lore priority for 801:

1st Weaponsmith — +20% attack for all military units
2nd Valhalla Reinforcement — Kàra regenerates health in high tide zones
3rd Feeling Safe — +3 Happiness with warchief, +1 per upgraded military camp
4th Fur Coats — Your units no longer have reduced power during winter
Year 802

Spectral Army & Military Build-Up

Goal: Build out your Spectral Warrior and Valkyrie force, push toward 500 Fame to recruit 4 more Spectral Warriors, and start dominating coastal engagements.

1
Maximize Spectral Warrior count
Keep your Wyrd balance negative and maintain multiple Hörgrs. Spectral Warriors are your primary military force — the more you have, the stronger your army.
2
Promote multiple Seeresses to Valkyries
Valkyries are powerful combat units. Combined with Kàra and Spectral Warriors, a full Valkyrie force is one of the strongest military compositions in the game.
3
Reach 500 Fame: 4 more Spectral Warriors
At 500 Fame, you recruit 4 additional Spectral Warriors. When your military units are killed in battle, they have a chance of returning as Spectral Warriors — making your army self-sustaining.
500 Fame
4
Push coastal engagements
Kraken is strongest in coastal zones. Push along the coast and use your Fishing Cabin network to sustain your army. Avoid non-coastal zones where your attack power is reduced by 30%.
⚔

Spectral resurrection: At 500 Fame, killed military units have a chance of returning as Spectral Warriors. This makes your army increasingly self-sustaining in extended campaigns — the more battles you fight, the more Spectral Warriors you accumulate.

Year 803

Sustained Army & Coastal Dominance

Goal: Sustain your Spectral Warrior and Valkyrie force, keep your Fishing Cabin economy running, and dominate coastal engagements.

1
Sustain Spectral Warriors + Valkyries
Keep Hörgrs running and maintain your Wyrd balance. With 500 Fame's resurrection mechanic, your army should be growing over time rather than shrinking.
2
Keep Kàra near Hörgr for healing
Kàra regenerates health slowly when standing next to the Hörgr or in a high tide zone. Keep her healthy between engagements — she's your most valuable combat unit.
3
Coordinate with team to close out
Kraken is a strong late-game carry on coastal maps. If your team has early-game clans, they should have created space for you to scale. Now it's time to deliver.
Year 804

Endgame Domination

Goal: Close out with Domination. Kraken's Spectral Warrior resurrection and Valkyrie force make them a formidable endgame threat on coastal maps.

1
Military surge with full Spectral + Valkyrie army
A full army of Spectral Warriors and Valkyries with Kàra is one of the most powerful military compositions in the game on coastal maps. Push for Domination victory.
2
Use Kraken's emergence for free feasts
At 200 Fame, you feast whenever the Kraken emerges. These free feasts provide production buffs without costing food — use them to sustain your army during the final push.
