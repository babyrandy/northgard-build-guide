Purpose

Who This Is For

This guide is built for two kinds of players:

⚔

Experienced players looking to pick up a new clan and understand its role, build order, and win conditions without having to piece it together from forum posts and YouTube videos.

🛡

New players just getting into the game who want a straightforward starting point — something that explains how a clan is normally played without overwhelming them with every edge case.

These guides are intentionally simple. The goal is to lead you down the right path, not cover every possible scenario. Once you understand the rhythm of a clan, you'll find your own line.
